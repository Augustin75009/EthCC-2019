EthCC 2019

About

EthCC 2019 (Ethereum Community Conference) is the second edition of a series of conferences and workshops that bring together and strengthen the international Ethereum community. EthCC will take place in Paris, on March 5th, 6th and 7th. EthCC makes a point of being inclusive. It is non-profit and the tickets are sold at cost price. Everybody is welcome, tech-savvy neophytes and experts, developers and non-techs, students and researchers, ....



During EthCC 2019 you will have the opportunity to:

  • Meet and listen to over 250 international speakers
  • Catch up with the latest developments on Ethereum
  • Have access to multiple tracks : Decentralized Finance, Scalability, Web 3.0, from Zero to Hero, ...
  • Learn new skills through workshops for developers & researchers
  • Network with passionate forethinkers
